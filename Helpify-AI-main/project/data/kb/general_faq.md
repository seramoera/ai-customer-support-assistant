# General FAQ

## Product Guidance
For product questions, ask what outcome the user wants, constraints, and timeline. Then provide options with tradeoffs.

## Travel Questions
For baggage and visa topics, ask route, destination, nationality, and travel dates before giving a final recommendation.

## Scheduling
For maintenance or reservation, collect preferred date/time, location, service details, and urgency.

## Ambiguous Questions
When intent is unclear, ask one focused clarification question instead of making assumptions.

## Open Questions
If a user asks broad or exploratory questions, provide a concise answer first, then suggest next best steps.
