# Support Policies

## Refunds
Customers can request refunds within policy windows. Verify purchase reference, date, and payment channel.

## Subscription Cancellation
Users can cancel subscriptions from account settings. Confirm cancellation date and whether access remains active until period end.

## Fraud and Account Security
If fraud is suspected, lock account access, rotate credentials, and escalate with evidence (timestamps, channels, and identifiers).

## Account Access
If users are blocked, validate identity and use secure recovery flow before unlocking the account.

## Escalation Guidelines
Escalate to a human specialist when legal, billing dispute, or high-risk security topics are involved.
